TopoJSONify: A Simple API

Upload a GeoJSON FeatureCollection or POST it to http://topojsonify.herokuapp.com/

  1. Upload GeoJSON



  2. POST

    POST http://topojsonify.herokuapp.com/

    POST directly in the body, or as a variable in the POST body

  3. Map

    Edit your data on http://topojsonify.herokuapp.com/map

    Drop GeoJSON or TopoJSON onto the map, draw and edit geometry, get back TopoJSON
